About Me

I’m a Senior AI/ML Engineer with over a decade of experience designing and deploying advanced machine learning systems across academia, industry, and startups. I’ve consistently delivered end-to-end ML products, led collaborations with cross-functional teams, and contributed to peer-reviewed research.
