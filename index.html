Jalal Arabneydi received his Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from McGill University, Montreal, Canada in 2017. He is currently a postdoctoral fellow at Concordia University. He was the recipient of the Best Student-Paper Award in the 53rd IEEE Conference on Decision and Control (CDC), 2014. His principal research interests include decentralized stochastic control, game and team theory, large-scale system, multi-agent reinforcement learning with applications in complex networks including smart grids, swarm robotics, and finance.
